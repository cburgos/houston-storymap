CIP Map Viewer: Draft 5-Year (2015 - 2020)

CIP Project Map Viewer uses Geographic Information Systems (GIS) technology to display location and information for hundreds of CIP projects that have been recently completed, are under construction, or planned for future construction over the next 5 years. The projects are divided into two categories: Street and Drainage and Water and Wastewater.

Note: This application was tested using IE 9, IE10, Firefox, Google Chrome and Samsung tablet

For questions regarding CIP Project Map viewer contact PUG.Helpdesk@houstontx.gov
